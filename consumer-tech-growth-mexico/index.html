Why it works

Utility has to become culture before it becomes demand.

A vacuum, processor, beauty device, laptop, or connected product does not win because the product sheet is elegant. It wins when people understand why it matters, see it used by credible people, trust the proof, and find a clear path to buy.

This is where performance and creators should stop fighting for budget and start acting like one system. Revolutionary concept. Apparently still rare.
