The work

For nearly a decade, Alexis Soubran has designed and operated growth systems for global brands across Mexico and Latin America. As CEO and Partner at Minimalist Agency, he helped position the firm as one of Mexico's leading independent growth partners — a Google Premier Partner, Meta Business Partner, and one of the first four TikTok Marketing Partners in Latin America. Under his leadership, the agency has operated growth for SharkNinja, AMD, Bitso, Edenred, Zeekr, Uber Eats, Airbnb, Miele, Nintendo, Motorola, and others.

His most recognized work is the 2019 Bitso campaign, which delivered a 400% increase in app conversions and 240% retention improvement through Universal App Campaigns. The work was nominated at the Google Premier Partner Awards in New York for Best App Campaign in Latin America. For SharkNinja, Soubran led a multi-product creator ecosystem in Mexico that delivered $21.7 CPA on CryoGlow, +662% search interest on Detect Pro, and +310% YoY e-commerce sales on Shark Beauty during Buen Fin.

His Fractional CMO practice serves a small number of global brands each year that need senior marketing leadership in LATAM without the cost or complexity of building a full regional team.

The framework

Alexis is the creator of Perfluencer Marketing, a methodology that fuses influencer marketing with performance marketing accountability. The framework — real-time ROAS dashboards, advanced audience segmentation, EMV calculation, Lift Studies, and Ethical Influence Guidelines — consistently delivers 4:1 ROAS, surpassing the industry standard of 1.5–3:1.

The complete framework is documented in his book Oops, I Bought It: What's Behind Influencer Marketing, available on Amazon.

The teaching

Alexis is a lecturer at Escuela de Mercadotecnia (EDEM) and faculty at Colectivo 23 / Kätedra. He has spoken at the Merca2.0 National Digital Marketing Congress and served as CMO of Tulum Crypto Fest 2024.

The music

Alexis is the vocalist, composer, and co-founder of Cavane, an indie rock band based in Mexico City. The band was formed in 2012 with his brother Irving Soubran and longtime friend Alejandro Escárcega. Cavane blends modern indie rock with the textures of 80s and 90s alternative — drawing influence from Zoé, Porcupine Tree, Pink Floyd, and Soda Stereo. Discography includes "MPM" (2022), "Hoy" (2022), "Solares" (2022), "Fly Away" (2024), and "Evil Until" (2025). A new EP releases in Q4 2026.
