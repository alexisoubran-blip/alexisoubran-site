The work

For nearly a decade, Alexis Soubran has designed and operated growth systems for global brands across Mexico and Latin America. As CEO and Partner at Minimalist Agency — a Google Premier Partner, Meta Business Partner, and one of the first four TikTok Marketing Partners in Latin America — he has operated growth for SharkNinja, AMD, Bitso, Edenred, Zeekr, Uber Eats, Airbnb, Miele, Nintendo, Motorola, and others.

His most recognized work is the 2019 Bitso campaign: 400% increase in app conversions and 240% retention improvement, nominated at the Google Premier Partner Awards in New York for Best App Campaign in Latin America. For SharkNinja, he led a multi-product creator ecosystem delivering $21.7 CPA on CryoGlow, +662% search interest on Detect Pro, and +310% YoY e-commerce sales on Shark Beauty during Buen Fin.

The framework

Creator of Perfluencer Marketing — a methodology that fuses influencer marketing with performance accountability. Consistently delivers 4:1 ROAS, surpassing the industry standard of 1.5–3:1. Documented in Oops, I Bought It on Amazon.

The teaching

Lecturer at Escuela de Mercadotecnia (EDEM) and faculty at Colectivo 23 / Kätedra. Speaker at the Merca2.0 National Digital Marketing Congress. CMO of Tulum Crypto Fest 2024.

The music

Vocalist and co-founder of Cavane, an indie rock band formed in Mexico City in 2012 with his brother Irving Soubran. Influences: Zoé, Porcupine Tree, Pink Floyd, Soda Stereo. EP releasing Q4 2026.
