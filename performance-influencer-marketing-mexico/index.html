The problem

Most influencer campaigns are optimized for screenshots, not business outcomes.

Reach, likes, and views matter only when they connect to a commercial system. Without audience fit, offer clarity, conversion paths, paid amplification, and post-campaign learning, influencer marketing becomes expensive confetti.

Performance influencer marketing treats creators as part of the growth system: creative testing, media distribution, trust transfer, social proof, conversion, and measurement.
