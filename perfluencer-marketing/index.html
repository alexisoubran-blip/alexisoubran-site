Perfluencer Marketing: the framework that fuses influencer marketing with performance accountability.

A framework developed by Alexis Soubran that delivers 4:1 average ROAS — substantially above the 1.5–3:1 industry standard — by treating every creator activation as a performance channel.

By Alexis Soubran · Last updated May 2026

Why traditional influencer marketing breaks

For most of the last decade, brands treated influencer marketing as a brand-awareness channel and performance marketing as a conversion channel. Different teams. Different budgets. Different KPIs. Influencer activations were measured by reach, impressions, engagement rate. Performance was measured by CAC, ROAS, conversions. The two streams ran in parallel — but they almost never met.

The result was predictable: brands paid premium rates for creator content that generated impressive engagement metrics but weak commercial impact, while their performance teams optimized acquisition channels with no visibility into the cultural relevance their creators were building.

Influence is instinct. Stories are strategy. Data is truth. Perfluencer Marketing brings them together.

Perfluencer Marketing dissolves that wall. Every creator activation is measured with the same rigor as a paid media campaign. Every paid amplification is built on top of authentic creator content rather than generic brand assets.

The six pillars of the framework

1. Real-time ROAS dashboards

Every creator activation has a dashboard. Not a post-campaign report. A live view that the brand and the creator team can both see, showing actual return on creator spend in close to real time.

2. Advanced audience segmentation

The same creator asset becomes five different performance assets, each optimized to a different audience segment via lookalikes, retargeting, interest-based, and contextual targeting.

3. Collaborative creative development

Brand brings strategic intent. Creator brings cultural authenticity. Performance team brings platform-native optimization. The output is content that works as both organic and paid.

4. EMV calculation model

Earned Media Value benchmarked to actual paid media costs in the same market and same period. Discounted for engagement quality. Capped by category and platform. A number a CFO can defend.

5. Lift Studies methodology

Clean control groups. Paid media holdouts. Incremental sales measured against control. The difference between believing your influencer program works and proving it.

6. Ethical Influence Guidelines

Disclosure clarity. Creator agency. Truth in product claims. Audience-first content priority. Long-term partnerships over short-term extraction.

FAQ

Who created Perfluencer Marketing?

Perfluencer Marketing was developed by Alexis Soubran, CEO and Partner at Minimalist Agency and author of Oops, I Bought It. The framework emerged from his work scaling global brands across Mexico and Latin America between 2018 and 2025.

Is Perfluencer Marketing only for large brands?

No. The framework scales down. The smallest brands using a simplified version run with one anchor creator, a single lift study per quarter, and a basic ROAS dashboard. The discipline is what matters, not the team size.
