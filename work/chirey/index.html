CHIREY OMODA JAECOO Data Architecture · Lead Acquisition · Measurement Ops

Operational map to capture, measure and convert leads

Process model to connect media, capture, lead quality, CRM, dealers, warehouse, attribution and operational governance. The objective is for Chirey, the agency and the dealer network to operate with one shared source of truth, clear quality criteria and actionable decisions.

Principle 1Commercial traceability requires an updated CRM, standardized lead status and connection to sales outcomes.
Principle 2The agency operates, measures and optimizes; Chirey enables data, access, compliance, business rules and decisions.
Principle 3Dealer response quality defines how much of the investment turns into real commercial opportunity.

Interactive view

Agency: execution, tracking, dashboards, optimization and tool operations.
Chirey: approvals, access, compliance, commercial data and governance.
Dealers: contact, follow-up, CRM, sales and feedback.
Shared: committee, scorecards, escalations and continuous improvement.

Working plan for the Chirey session

The session should translate the ecosystem into daily operations: what is delivered, when it is delivered, who validates it, what evidence is kept, what data the agency needs and which decisions Chirey must enable to operate consistently.

01

Media Operations and Reporting

Meeting check
  • Monthly media proposal format: investment, channel, flight, objective, KPI, asset, market, model and owner.
  • Timings: proposal, validation, campaign setup, QA, weekly follow-up and monthly close.
  • Evidence: platform screenshots, IOs/orders, active links, QR codes, UTMs, platform captures and approved changes.
  • Monthly report: initial proposal vs. actual execution vs. billed amount vs. performance.
Platforms / supports
  • Google Ads, Meta Ads, TikTok Ads, OOH, DOOH, TV, media plan matrix, Looker Studio and BigQuery.
02

Lead Optimization and Quality

Meeting check
  • Tools: Seekop CRM, Biky.ai, Website Forms, WhatsApp, Captcha, ClickCease and dashboards.
  • Quality criteria: unique lead, contactable, valid model/market, consent, traceable source and updated status.
  • Monitoring: CPL, CPLQ, contact rate, speed to lead, appointment, show, quote, sale and lost reasons.
  • Seekop triggers: lead_created, assigned_to_dealer, first_contact, appointment_set, test_drive, quote, sold, lost.
Critical condition
  • Optimization must connect with CRM quality and stage progression, not only platform CPL.
03

Tools, Access and Integration

Meeting check
  • Chirey access: Looker Studio, reports, media matrix, campaign status, action tracker and BigQuery views.
  • Agency access: ad platforms, GA4, GTM, website/landing pages, Seekop exports/API, WhatsApp, Funnel/Supermetrics, SegmentStream, Hawky AI and Make.
  • Required data: inventory, promotions, vehicle offers, active dealers, sales, appointments, quotes and billing.
  • Integration: unique lead ID, source of truth, BigQuery data schema, permissions and trigger documentation.
Critical condition
  • Data must live in the warehouse with update, cleaning and ownership rules by source.
04

Administration and Team

Meeting check
  • Monthly billing: reconciliation of approved proposal vs. contracted media vs. executed media vs. billed amount.
  • Cutoffs: evidence deadline, discrepancy review, approval and invoice issuance.
  • Team: org chart, main contact, escalation paths and owners for media, data, creative, account, finance and leadership.
  • Cadence: weekly operations meeting, monthly business review and financial close.
Critical condition
  • A decision log must exist so investment, asset or process changes remain traceable.

Recommended implementation roadmap · 6 months

Month 1 · Diagnosis and complianceInventory of access, platforms, privacy policies, vendors, forms, CRM, current flows and dealer network.
Month 2 · Taxonomy and instrumentationNaming, UTMs, QR/links, GTM, GA4, events, data layer, media matrix and offline tracking/MAID requirements.
Month 3 · CRM + warehouse pilotSeekop triggers, BigQuery schema, connectors, base dashboards and pilot with prioritized dealers.
Month 4 · Offline conversions and attributionGoogle Offline/EC, Meta CAPI/Offline, SegmentStream, attribution models and matching field validation.
Month 5 · Dealer rolloutProgressive scaling to approximately 90 dealers, training, SLA, scorecards and CRM update audits.
Month 6 · Governance and optimizationOperating committee, monthly reconciliation, final dashboards, Brandintelle, action tracker and continuous optimization backlog.

Operational consideration: due to compliance, integrations, commercial data, offline conversions and a network of approximately 90 dealers, the rollout must be phased. The priority is to secure adoption, traceability and data quality before scaling automation and advanced attribution.
