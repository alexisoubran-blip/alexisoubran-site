The challenge

Bitso, the largest crypto exchange in Latin America, needed to scale app installs and active retention in a market with high volatility, low category awareness, and intense competition. The bottleneck was not awareness — it was the gap between download and meaningful in-app engagement.

The strategy

  • Optimize for in-app value events, not installs. Universal App Campaigns restructured to bid against deep-funnel events.
  • Audience layering by financial behavior. Custom audiences built around financial product affinity signals.
  • Creative iteration at high cadence. Rapid testing across formats with a feedback loop to performance data.

The results

Within 30 days of the new campaign architecture going live, Bitso achieved a 35% increase in app installs. By the end of the campaign cycle, conversions had increased 400% and retention had improved 240%. Monthly registrations grew 5x.

The campaign was nominated at the Google Premier Partner Awards in New York for Best App Campaign in Latin America — recognition reserved for the top 3% of agencies globally.

Why it mattered

Bitso became the proof case for the approach later codified as Perfluencer Marketing — measuring every marketing investment against business outcomes, not channel-level vanity metrics.
