The challenge

SharkNinja entered Mexico with a deep product portfolio — kitchen appliances, home cleaning, and beauty tech — but limited brand awareness. The standard playbook (product-by-product campaigns, each with its own creator pool) was too expensive to scale and too fragmented to build a coherent brand.

The strategy

Functional storytelling

Content that demonstrated what the products actually do, in real domestic contexts, with results visible in the frame. In a category saturated with aspirational imagery, demonstrated functionality is the most differentiated signal.

Creator portfolio architecture

Three tiers: anchor creators (long-term partners across multiple products), product specialists (deep affinity for a specific category), and cultural amplifiers (brought the brand into adjacent conversations).

Perfluencer Marketing measurement

Every activation instrumented under the Perfluencer Marketing framework — real-time ROAS dashboards, audience segmentation for paid amplification of organic content, and lift-study-based attribution.

The results

  • CryoGlow: $21.7 CPA via TikTok Ads + influencer marketing — below all category benchmarks.
  • Detect Pro: +662% search interest via integrated creator + paid amplification.
  • Shark Beauty (Buen Fin): +310% e-commerce sales YoY. 6.5M interactions across earned + paid during peak retail.
